Voxman Music Building

The Voxman Music Building, located on the corner of Clinton and Burlington Street, is the new home for the University of Iowa’s School of Music. Designed by LMN Architects from Seattle, the space features a 700-seat concert hall, 200-seat recital hall, organ performance hall, opera and chamber rehearsal spaces, faculty studios, classrooms, and individual rehearsal spaces. This new facility represents an incredible and unwavering commitment to music on the part of alumni, donors, faculty, students, and staff at the University of Iowa.
